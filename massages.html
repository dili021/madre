Therapeutic Massage

Therapeutic massage is a general term that describes any type of massage modality that helps a specific problem. It is not only a highly beneficial therapy, but also known as one of the healthiest options for improving one’s quality of life.

Therapeutic Massage could be a relevant choice of health care in the following areas:
- Promotes and maintains good health – promotes tissue health and encourages health awareness.
- Stress management – helps relieve associated muscular tension and general relaxation.
- Post-operative care – helps reduce recovery period.
- Emotional and /or psychological disorders – releases endorphins that help to reduce depression.
- Chronic pain – helps break the pain spasm cycle by reducing muscle tightness.

The different therapeutic massage techniques are extremely beneficial in the treatment of rheumatic disorders and in the recovery and maintenance of the functional capacity.

Lymph Drainage

Lymphatic massage is commonly referred to as lymphatic drainage. It is a gentle, rhythmical massage performed by a specially trained therapist to stimulate the circulation of lymph around the body. This helps to rapidly speed up the removal of wastes and toxins from a sluggish lymphatic system.

Lymphatic massage can be helpful for prevention of swelling after injury or surgery. It is also thought to provide a major boost to the immune system. Lymphatic Drainage aims to increase the efficiency of your lymphatic and circulatory system. By reducing the volume of retained fluid and the pressure associated, it can reduce the risk of impeded circulation. It has also been shown to be beneficial in post-operative conditions.

Pregnancy Massage

Massage during pregnancy has gained a lot of popularity in the western world in recent years. It is known as a valid and useful way to reduce pregnancy discomforts. However, it is really important to find a qualified therapist.

- When done effectively, it reduces stress hormones within a pregnant woman’s body. It also increases the level of hormones which tend to be low when women are depressed.
- Improved sleep due to feeling less muscle tension and generally less uncomfortable.
- It can improve the progression of labour with better health outcomes for both the mother and the baby.
- Relieves the everyday discomforts of pregnancy, such as an aching neck, sore back…
- Leads to muscle relaxation and relieves joint pain.
- Gives an opportunity to pregnant women to get some rest and completely relax.

Relax Massage

Relax massages are smooth, gentle, and flowing. They promote general relaxation, relieve muscular tension, and improve circulation and range of movement. However, relax massages are less effective than the deeper styles that work with soft tissue.
