Yoga

Yoga is an ancient system of physical and mental practices or disciplines which originated in ancient India. Yoga means Union. The fundamental purpose of yoga is to foster harmony in the body, mind, and soul. Yoga professes a complete system of physical, mental, social, and spiritual development.
Yoga uses breathing techniques (Pranayama), exercise (Asana) and meditation. It’s more than physical exercise; it has a meditative and spiritual core, and helps to improve health and happiness.
There is no age restriction or mobility limit for practicing yoga.

Some of benefits of practicing Yoga are:

  • Healthy heart – hypertension
  • Better bone density – osteoporosis
  • Builds core strength – injury
  • Lubricates the joints – joint mobility
  • Treats a backache – lower backpain and other back problems
  • Improves balance and posture
  • Relieves stress
  • Enhances quality of Prana – energy flow
  • Overall well-being – A regular practice of Yoga enhances multiple aspects of physical, mental and spiritual being.
